Past vs. Present 過去與現在：生活大轉變

Let's find out how everyday toys, schools, and journeys look different today! 讓我們一起探索，你身邊的玩具、學校還有交通工具在以前和現在有什麼不一樣！

Toys & Playtime / 玩具與遊戲時間

🪵

The Past / 以前

Kids played with simple wooden blocks, hand-sewn dolls, and spinning tops. They used active imaginations!

以前的小朋友玩木頭積木、手縫玩偶和陀螺。他們用滿滿的想像力玩耍！
🎮

The Present / 現在

Now, we have video games and tablets. You can play colorful digital worlds and solve puzzles with a single tap!

現在我們有電子遊戲和平板電腦。點一下就能在彩色的世界裡玩遊戲！

Classrooms / 學校課堂與學習

📝

Classic Classrooms / 以前的教室

Students had wooden benches, blackboard slates, and heavy paper books. Writing was done with real ink!

以前的小朋友坐木頭長椅，看黑板。要用真正的墨水在紙本上寫字！
💻

Smart Classrooms / 現在的教室

Today, we learn with interactive smart boards, colorful computers, and playful learning apps!

現在，我們用觸控電子白板、電腦和好玩的學習APP來上課！

Traveling to School / 我們怎麼去上學？

Long Walks of the Past / 以前：走長長的路

Years ago, kids walked for miles across fields, or rode in slow horse-drawn carriages to get to class.

以前的小朋友要穿過原野走好幾公里的路，或者坐慢吞吞的馬車去學校！

Comfortable Modern Bus / 現在：舒適的校車

Today, safe, bright buses and cars whisk us directly to the school doors in warmth and comfort!

現在，明亮又溫暖的安全校車或汽車，可以直接把我們舒舒服服地送到校門口！

Talking to Friends / 如何跟好朋友聯絡？

1. Paper Letters / 手寫信件

People wrote letters by hand! It took days or weeks for a mail carrier to deliver them.

以前要一筆一劃寫信，郵差叔叔要花好幾天甚至星期才能送到！

2. Rotary Phones / 撥號電話

Later, houses shared heavy dial-up phones connected by long wires. No texting, only calling!

後來，家裡有了有線大電話，要用手指轉圈撥號。那時不能傳訊息喔！

3. Video Calls / 即時視訊

Today, we can video chat instantly, send funny emojis, and talk face-to-face from anywhere!

現在我們可以隨時用平板或手機面對面視訊，還能傳可愛的貼圖！
