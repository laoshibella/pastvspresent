Toys & Playtime / 玩具與遊戲時間

🪵 Retro Play

The Past / 以前

Children long ago played with simple toys crafted from wood, clay, or fabric. They relied on active imaginations!

以前的小朋友玩用木頭、黏土或布料手做的簡單玩具。全靠豐富想像力玩耍！
🎮 Digital Era

The Present / 現在

Today, we play with video consoles, robotic coding pets, and smart tablets with vibrant pixel graphics!

現在的我們玩電視遊戲機、程式設計機器人、還有彩色螢幕的平板！

Classrooms & Study / 學校課堂與學習

📝

Classic Slate & Ink / 以前的教室與紙筆

Students wrote on blackboard slabs with chalk, or dipped fine metal quills into wet ink!

以前的同學在小黑板石板上寫字，或者用羽毛筆蘸著墨水寫字！

💻

Smart Touchscreens / 現在的智慧課堂

Today's classrooms use interactive projectors and educational apps that turn science into a playground!

現在的教室有大大的觸控智慧白板，還能用平板玩學習APP！

Getting to School / 我們怎麼去上學？

Long Trails of the Past / 以前：走路或搭馬車

Children walked miles over dirt paths or rode bumpy horse carriages.

以前的小朋友走泥濘小路，或搭晃晃的慢馬車。

Fast and Cozy Bus / 現在：安全的黃色校車

Today, electric buses or cars carry us directly to school safely.

現在有溫暖的大黃色校車、捷運或汽車接送。

Talking to Friends / 如何跟好朋友聯絡？

1. Paper Mail / 手寫紙本信件

Carriers traveled by foot or horseback to deliver them over weeks!

以前寫在信紙上，由郵差送好幾個星期。

2. Rotary Phone / 轉盤電話

Heavy devices sat in living rooms. You could only hear voice, no texting!

後來有了重重的轉盤電話，只能通話不能傳簡訊！

3. Video Chat / 即時網路視訊

Instantly dial high-definition video calls with friends worldwide!

現在隨時用手機開視訊，一秒看見世界各地的朋友！
