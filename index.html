Present vs. Past

現在式對比過去式

Present (現在)

Happening now or regularly.

正在發生或規律發生的事。

💡 Clue: Today, Everyday

Past (過去)

Already finished and done.

已經結束並完成的事。

💡 Clue: Yesterday, Last night

Regular Verbs (+ed)

規則動詞（字尾加 ed）

Today (Present)

I walk to school.

我走路去上學。

She plays games.

她玩遊戲。

Yesterday (Past)

I walked to school.

我昨天走路去上學。

She played games.

她昨天玩了遊戲。

Irregular Rebels!

不規則變形動詞！

Present (Every day)

We eat apples.

我們吃蘋果。

They run fast.

他們跑很快。

Past (Last week)

We ate apples.

我們上週吃了蘋果。

They ran fast.

他們之前跑很快。
